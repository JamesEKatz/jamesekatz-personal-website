About

James E. Katz is a distinguished academic with extensive experience in communication studies. His research focuses on the social aspects of communication technologies. He has published numerous papers in top-tier journals and has been a keynote speaker at various international conferences.

Publications

  • "Living inside mobile social information" - Editor, 2013
  • "The social media president: Barack Obama and the politics of digital engagement" - Co-author, 2013
  • "Mobile communication: Dimensions of social policy" - Editor, 2011
